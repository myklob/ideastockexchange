🎤 Rate & Debate Media

Over 2,400 years ago, Plato warned that storytelling and music can "find their way into the inmost soul and take strongest hold upon it" — teaching values more effectively than reason ever could. He was right. One blockbuster film reaches 100 million people in weeks. The best academic paper on the same topic reaches 500. The ISE tracks which media is winning which debates, and how honestly.

The Influence Ratio: Why This Matters

The entertainment track wins by orders of magnitude — regardless of truth content. That's not a minor problem. It's a civilizational-scale distortion of collective intelligence.
