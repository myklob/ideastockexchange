Rep. Confidence = how accurately this group's interests are captured in ISE data. Power = average of Political / Economic / Military / Narrative / Institutional scores (see each profile for breakdown).

Key insight on power vs. legitimacy: Stakeholders with the lowest power scores often hold the highest-validity interests (e.g., civilian populations bear the greatest cost of conflict but have the least ability to stop it). The ISE framework scores these dimensions separately — power determines who can force an outcome; validity determines whose interests should carry the most moral weight. Both matter; neither cancels the other.
